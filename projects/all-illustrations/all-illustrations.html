Flexii - Illustrations for Team Values

As requested by the founders of Flexii, I was requested to produce a series of posters depicting the 4 values Flexii as a team aims to adhere to, which was part of the new MVV (Mission, Vision and Value) which I helped in co-developing after spending a few months in the company with the help of our freelance brand designer.

The illustrations were created based on the chick mascot of Flexii, with various different scenes that depicts communicates the value itself while at the same time, stays in line with the brand visual direction of being playful and energetic. The 4 posters were thereafter printed on canvas and displayed in the office for team members and company visitors to better understand or be reminded of the core values of Flexii.
